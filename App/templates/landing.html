Surf Through Your Emotions!



In a world where people's well-being is increasingly connected to their emotional health, 'Wave' emerges as a practical solution to help individuals manage their emotions and mental states. Wave makes it easier for people to understand and deal with their emotions effectively. This project was inspired by the need to address emotional well-being and is based on the concept of using technology to support mental health.
